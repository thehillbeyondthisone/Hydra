How to Find Your Anarchy Online Logs

Windows Path:

C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Local\Funcom\Anarchy Online\[UniqueID]\Anarchy Online\Prefs\[CharacterName]\[CharacterID]\Chat\Windows\Window[X]\Log.txt

Quick Instructions:

  1. Press Win + R to open Run dialog
  2. Type: %localappdata%\Funcom\Anarchy Online
  3. Navigate through folders: find your character's Chat folder
  4. Look in Windows\Window[number] folders for active Log.txt files
  5. Drag and drop the Log.txt file into a sensor above, or click the dropzone

üí° Tip: The active chat log is usually in Window0, Window1, or Window2. Look for the largest or most recently modified Log.txt file.
